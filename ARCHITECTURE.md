# Cognitive Enterprise Twin Architecture

## Overview

The Cognitive Enterprise Twin (CET) is a Multi-Agent Decision Intelligence Platform designed for Small and Medium-Sized Enterprises (SMEs).

Unlike traditional business intelligence dashboards, the platform combines enterprise memory, specialized executive AI agents, strategic debate, and decision intelligence to support business growth, revenue optimization, and risk-aware decision making.

The platform aims to function as a digital cognitive twin of an SME by continuously analysing business data, identifying opportunities, detecting risks, and generating executive-level recommendations.

# Enterprise Memory Layer

The Enterprise Memory Layer serves as the organizational memory of the system.

### Stores

* Previous datasets
* Historical insights
* Business risks
* Revenue opportunities
* Strategic decisions
* Agent recommendations

### Benefits

* Long-term organizational learning
* Decision tracking
* Pattern recognition over time
* Continuous improvement

---

# Strategic Debate Layer

The Strategic Debate Layer enables agents to challenge and validate recommendations before final decisions are generated.

### Example

Revenue Agent:

Increase investment in Product A.

Risk Officer Agent:

Product A depends heavily on a single customer segment.

Strategy Agent:

Controlled investment is recommended with diversification.

CEO Agent:

Recommendation approved with medium-risk classification.

# Decision Intelligence Framework

The platform aims to answer five core business questions:

### What Happened?

Historical analysis.

### Why Did It Happen?

Root cause analysis.

### What Could Happen Next?

Forecasting and prediction.

### What Are the Risks?

Risk identification and assessment.

### What Should We Do?

Prescriptive strategic recommendations.
