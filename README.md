# Cognitive Enterprise Twin

## A Multi-Agent Cognitive Enterprise Twin for Strategic Decision Intelligence and Revenue Optimization in SMEs

Cognitive Enterprise Twin is an advanced AI decision intelligence prototype designed for small and medium-sized enterprises. The platform uses multiple executive-style AI agents to analyse business data, identify risks, discover revenue opportunities, simulate strategic options, and generate explainable recommendations.

## Core Concept

Traditional dashboards show what happened.

Cognitive Enterprise Twin goes further by helping decision-makers understand:

- What happened?
- Why did it happen?
- What could happen next?
- What should the business do?

## Key Innovation

This project introduces a Cognitive Enterprise Twin model that combines:

- Multi-agent AI reasoning
- Enterprise memory
- Strategic debate between agents
- Risk-aware recommendation generation
- Revenue optimization
- Executive decision reporting
- SME-focused decision intelligence

## Core Agents

- Data Scientist Agent
- Revenue Optimization Agent
- Risk Officer Agent
- Strategy Agent
- CEO Agent

## Version 1 Features

- Upload SME business datasets
- Analyse revenue, customer, and product performance
- Detect business risks
- Identify growth opportunities
- Generate executive-level recommendations
- Provide explainable AI decision summaries

## Long-Term Vision

The long-term goal is to develop a research-grade AI platform that acts as a digital cognitive twin of an SME, continuously learning from business data, previous decisions, risks, and outcomes to support better strategic decision-making.
